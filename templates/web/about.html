About - Us

W elcome to our organic food store and bakery in Kondotty, Kerala! We take pride in offering a wide range of high-quality, organic food products that are sure to delight your taste buds. Our shelves are stocked with a variety of delicious pickles, including lemon pickles, mango pickles, and an assortment of other organic pickles.

As a Kudumbashree product, we are committed to supporting local communities and promoting sustainable practices. When you choose our products, you're not only indulging in flavorful treats but also contributing to a meaningful cause.

We understand the importance of hygiene, which is why we prioritize it in every aspect of our business. You can trust that our products are prepared in a clean and safe environment, ensuring the highest quality and freshness.
